LT XLPE Insulated Power Cable

LT XLPE Insulated Power Cables provide reliable and long-lasting power transmission and distribution. The XLPE insulation offers superior resistance to heat, moisture, chemicals, and mechanical stress. Cross-linking enhances thermal stability, reducing risks. Available in various configurations, these cables ensure efficient power transmission with high dielectric strength and low loss. They also prioritize safety with low smoke and zero halogen properties.

FEATURES

Material

LT XLPE Insulated Power Cables are made from premium aluminium.

Configuration

LT XLPE Insulated Power Cables are available in multiple stranded configurations.

Size and Thickness

LT XLPE Insulated Power Cables are available in various ranges between 1.5 sqmm-100sqmm. The insulation materials used are made using cross linked polyethylene insulation by extrusion.The outer sheath is made using PVC conforming to type ST2 Compound (IS5031-1984)

Standards

LT XLPE Insulated Power Cables conform to IS 7098 Part 1: 1998

Voltage Rating

LT XLPE Insulated Power Cables are suitable for use on AC Single phase or 3 phase (earthed or unearthed) systems for rated voltage upto and including 1100 volts. These may also be used on AC Systems for rated voltages upto and including 1500 volts to earth.

Temperature rating

LT XLPE Insulated Power Cables can operate in temperatures ranging between 90 degrees celsius - 250 degree celsius (under normal and short circuit conditions)

APPLICATIONS

High-Voltage Power Transmission

LT XLPE Insulated Power Cables are extensively used in high-voltage power transmission systems due to their excellent dielectric properties. They can withstand high voltages and currents, ensuring reliable and efficient power transmission over long distances. LT XLPE Insulated Power Cables are often used in underground and overhead transmission lines, reducing power losses and minimising environmental impact.

Industrial Automation and Control Systems

In industrial automation and control systems, LT XLPE Insulated Power Cables LT XLPE Insulated Power Cablesare used to connect various components, such as sensors, actuators, and control panels. Their flexibility, durability, and resistance to oil, chemicals, and heat make them suitable for harsh industrial environments. LT XLPE Insulated Power Cables ensure reliable and efficient data transmission in industrial automation systems.

Railway and Transportation Systems

LT XLPE Insulated Power Cables are used in railway and transportation systems to power signaling systems, track circuits, and train control systems. Their resistance to vibration, impact, and extreme temperatures makes them suitable for the demanding conditions of railway environments. LT XLPE Insulated Power Cables ensure reliable and safe operation of railway systems.

Renewable energy

LT XLPE Insulated Power Cables connect solar panels and wind turbines to inverters and the grid. Their durability against harsh conditions makes them ideal for outdoor installations in renewable energy systems, ensuring efficient and reliable energy transfer.
