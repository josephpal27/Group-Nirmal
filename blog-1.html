50 Years of Group Nirmal

Group Nirmal has an expansive product range comprising over 100 SKUs of different products including Galvanized and Black Steel Wires, Aluminium Conductors, Ribbed Wires, Welding Electrodes, and Hot Rolled Steel products. Excerpts of interaction with MD Nirmal Saraf, Nirmal Wires Pvt. Ltd. – Group Nirmal.

Group Nirmal is a Leading Manufacturer of World-Class Steel Wires, boasting an annual production capacity of 180,000 metric tons and a revenue of 1200 crores. With 5 cutting-edge manufacturing facilities, our main offerings include galvanized and ungalvanized steel wires, aluminum conductors, and pre-stressed concrete wires.

We are committed to driving innovation while promoting sustainability and ethical standards. Our vision is to create value for all stakeholders, including suppliers, customers, business partners, and society at large. Based in Kolkata, we aim to revive West Bengal’s status as a leading producer of steel products and establish the “NIRMAL” brand through continuous improvement, cost-effective high-quality production, and exceeding customer expectations.

Reflecting on the Milestones of Group Nirmal’s Endeavor:

The story of Group Nirmal began in 1971 under the leadership of Shri Ramautar Saraf. Driven by the passion to provide the highest quality steel products in West Bengal, he established a small wire-drawing unit as part of Central Store Supplies (P) Ltd., a corporation founded by his late father Shri Jhabarmal Saraf in 1942. Despite numerous challenges, Shri Ramautar Saraf persevered and in 1985 established a galvanized steel wire plant under the entity Nirmal Wires Pvt. Ltd, established in 1981.

Through hard work and determination, Group Nirmal has grown into a thriving enterprise in the steel products industry, serving customers across 40 countries globally. Our offerings have expanded to over 100 different products, including galvanized and black steel wires, aluminium conductors, ribbed wires, welding electrodes, and hot-rolled steel products. Our wires are used in a range of applications, including producing umbrella ribs, springs, earthing wires, staying wires, cable armouring, and concrete reinforcement wires. They are utilized in industries such as power generation and transmission, security and protection, fencing, general engineering, automotive, and more.

To serve retail customers, Group Nirmal has established a comprehensive distribution network. This network caters to a broad range of consumers in the agricultural sector and household sector, providing them with chain link fence, barbed wire, and galvanized wire, among others. The retail segment also caters to the small requirements of industrial customers, offering welding consumables and wire nettings, available at our retail counters.

As a solution provider in the Wire and Cable industry, Group Nirmal sets itself apart by offering not only quality products but also exceptional after-sales service and a robust customer feedback system. Recognized for our innovative approach, we customize our offerings to meet the unique needs of our customers, all while prioritizing sustainability in our production processes. Our dedicated quality team is committed to ensuring the impeccable quality of our finished products, aligned with our vision of creating value for all stakeholders. These factors have earned Group Nirmal the loyalty and trust of our customers, solidifying our commitment to quality.

The Path Forward:

As Group Nirmal moves into the future, we have recently introduced new products, including ASTM Class C and Class B Zinc Coated wires, which are in limited production globally. Our product offerings continue to evolve with plans for the development of aluminium clad wires, low relaxation pre-stressed concrete wires, and patented steel wires.

In line with India’s vision of self-reliance, we are committed to contributing to the country’s goal of being “Aatma Nirbhar.” To achieve this, the wire and cable industry must strive to develop and manufacture products that can substitute imports and attain a global leadership position.

With competitive pricing and unparalleled quality, we believe that India has the potential to become a dominant player in the global supply chain of steel and steel products. International economic conditions are favourable, and customers are open to Indian products. As we work hard to capitalize on this opportunity, we remain steadfast in our commitment to “Make in India” and Make for the World.
