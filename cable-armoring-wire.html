Cable Armoring Wire

Cable Armoring wires safeguard cables from damage, environmental factors, and interference. Made of high-tensile materials like galvanized steel, they are wound or braided around the cable, providing robust protection. This enhances durability, resistance to impact, abrasion, and rodent damage. It also shields against EMI/RFI, ensuring optimal signal transmission. The wire's design balances protection and flexibility for diverse installations.

FEATURES

Material

Cable Armoring wires are made from premium steel wire rods and are hot dip galvanised with zinc.

Standards

Cable Armoring wires conform to IS 3975

Tensile Strength

Tensile strength of Cable Armoring wires ranges between 350-450 MPa

Wire Diameter Range

The wire diameter range of Cable Armoring wires ranges between 1.4-4.00 mm

Packaging

Cable Armoring wires are packaged in coils.

APPLICATIONS

Armoring of cables

Cable armoring wire safeguards the cable from mechanical damage like abrasion, crushing and bending. Additionally, it also shields the cable against electromagnetic and radio frequency interference. Armored cables are commonly used in demanding environments such as construction sites, industrial settings, and underground installations.
