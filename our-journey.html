Acquisition of CeeDee Metalloys

Acquisition of CeeDee Metalloys in Bangalore. With an annual production capacity of 6000 MT, this manufacturing facility added Zinc Wires, Zinc-Aluminum Wires and Zinc Wire rods to our product portfolio.

2024

Acquisition of Khordha Plant

Acquisition of Hindustan Urban Infrastructure’s Khordha unit. This plant with an annual production capacity of 30,000 MT added Aluminum Wire rods to our portfolio & expands our capacity of Aluminum Conductor production.
