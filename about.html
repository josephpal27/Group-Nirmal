ABOUT US

Group Nirmal, founded in 1971 by Shree Ram Autar Saraf, is a leading manufacturer of steel, aluminum, and zinc wires with an annual turnover exceeding ₹2000 crores and a production capacity of 216,000 metric tons. We serve various industries such as power, defense, and agriculture, while also trading metals to support India's infrastructure development. With over 50 years of experience and exports to more than 40 countries, we prioritize quality and innovation, adhering to national and international standards, positioning us as a leader in the Indian wire and cable industry.
