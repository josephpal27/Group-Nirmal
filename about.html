ABOUT US

Founded in 1971 by Shree Ram Autar Saraf, Group Nirmal has evolved from a modest aspiration into a substantial enterprise, with a valuation of 2000 crores. Our dedication to excellence is reflected in our production of world-class steel wires, aluminum wires and conductors, zinc wires, and an extensive range of over 40 products. With a robust annual production capacity exceeding 200,000 metric tons, we have consistently expanded our operations while maintaining a strong commitment to sustainable practices. Beyond manufacturing, we are actively engaged in the trading of high-quality metals and contributing to India's infrastructure development.

Being at the forefront of the industry, Nirmal has become a testament to the enduring synergy between quality and innovation. Our 50+ years of experience in manufacturing, exporting, and wholesaling a diverse array of industrial products is what defines our unwavering commitment to quality and innovation. Our comprehensive product portfolio, which includes GI wires & strands, aluminum wires & aluminum conductors, PC Wires & strands, fencing products, and welding electrodes, adheres strictly to industry standards.

BRAND STORY

More than a brand, Nirmal is a legacy built on the foundation of quality, innovation, and customer centricity. Right from our motto “Re imagineering Tomorrow” which shows our will to re-imagine and re-engineer tomorrow to our logo which represents a passion for growth and diversification, Nirmal is a spirit of relentless pursuit to achieve excellence, striving to create a better future for all.

Rooted deep into our culture of innovation is a mindset where challenges are viewed as opportunities for growth. This is what fuels our dedication to delivering cutting-edge solutions that exceed customer expectations, with quality being a crucial part of what we offer.

Our unwavering dedication to producing world-class products has earned us a reputation for reliability and trust. This commitment to excellence extends beyond the manufacturing process as it is what defines every aspect of our business, from research and development to customer service.

Our customer-centric approach is also a major part of our brand’s identity. We believe in building long-term partnerships based on mutual trust and understanding. Nirmal represents a harmonious blend of tradition and innovation, quality and care, and a steadfast commitment towards customer satisfaction and building relationships.
