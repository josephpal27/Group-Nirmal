CSR

At Group Nirmal, we believe in building more than just products; we believe in building stronger communities. It's a conviction that guides us, especially in the areas surrounding our production units in Deulti, Khurda, and Bangalore. For us, our CSR initiatives are a personal commitment, a way to give back to the places we call home and contribute to the nation's progress. We believe in creating meaningful change through initiatives that touch lives directly and address unique community needs to create a lasting impact. We invite you to explore our initiatives below and discover the ways we are making a difference, and to join us in our journey of building a stronger, more sustainable tomorrow for India.

Our Initiatives

We strive continuously to support community well-being across a range of areas, with the hope of contributing to inclusive progress and resolving various issues. Through our efforts, we seek to address various challenges and promote equitable growth and upliftment, with the intent of building a foundation where more individuals can prosper and contribute to a resilient future.

Education -

Illiteracy is the root cause behind several pressing social concerns like poverty in India, often perpetuated by lack of access to educational institutions and resources. Recognizing this, we took the following initiatives to empower communities through making education more accessible for them.

MANKUR NORTH PRIMARY SCHOOL & DWIPMALITA PRIMARY SCHOOL

In collaboration with Roundtable India, we focused on enhancing the learning environments of 2 schools near our manufacturing unit in Deulti, West Bengal. At Mankur North Primary School (Deulti), 4 classrooms were built and essential furniture like benches and chairs were provided. Another significant upgrade was made at the Dwipmalita Primary School in Nadia, where 6 new classrooms were constructed. These efforts aim to provide students with improved facilities, fostering a more conducive atmosphere for learning.

BAGNAN COLLEGE

In Bagnan College, where skill development was hampered by resource constraints, we donated 15 desktop computers to establish a computer lab, which has helped several students update themselves with crucial digital literacy skills, bridge the digital divide and expand their skills and learning opportunities. We also donated 15 sewing machines, enabling students to acquire practical skills that can prove to be crucial for their entrepreneurial ventures to enhance their financial independence.

EKAL VIDYALAYA- FRIENDS OF TRIBAL SOCIETY

Partnering with the Friends of Tribal Society, we now support the “Ekal Vidyalaya” movement, a remarkable 1 teacher school initiative. Presently, we are actively supporting 51 Ekal Vidyalayas across West Bengal that help children from underserved regions pursue educational opportunities and achieve basic literacy.

Healthcare -

In a nation where healthcare infrastructure often falls short, countless individuals face the harsh outcome of limited access to essential medical services. Recognizing this, we've taken several initiatives to strengthen healthcare support within communities, focusing on improving access and affordability.

COVID VACCINE DRIVE- HEAD OFFICE AND DEULTI

During the COVID-19 outbreak, we conducted vaccination drives for our employees at our head office and Deulti unit. The program was extended to the families and communities surrounding our Deulti unit, prioritizing the health and well-being of our workforce while also contributing to a broader community within the regions of our operation.

CERVICAL CANCER VACCINE DRIVE – MARWARI RELEIF SOCIETY

In collaboration with the century-old Marwari Relief Society, we organized a vaccine drive against cervical cancer, a disease that disproportionately affects women and presents a significant public health challenge. Our contribution included 200 vaccines towards this crucial health program, with an aim to promote access to preventative care and reduce the prevalence of this disease within the community, directly addressing a critical need for vulnerable populations.

PROSTHETIC LIMBS- NARAYAN SEVA SANSTHAN

Partnering with the Narayan Seva Sansthan we donated prosthetic limbs to empower the differently abled. This initiative restored mobility, independence, and social and economic inclusion. We aimed to break down barriers, enabling individuals to reclaim societal roles. We believe this will promote a future where healthcare is accessible, and communities thrive.

Sports -

Sports is a powerful means to foster physical and mental well-being. It cultivates discipline, teamwork and resilience, teaching young individuals to set goals, persevere, and cooperate, thereby shaping a strong character. Recognizing this profound impact, we have undertaken several initiatives to promote and make sports more accessible, particularly for the youth.

Bada Bazar Yuvak Sabha

Supporting the Barabazar Yuvak Sabha is another example demonstrating our dedication to community empowerment. Established in 1923, the club seeks to support and empower talented athletes who lack financial resources to pursue their passion. Their volleyball team consistently participates and excels in various state leagues and knockout championships. They have secured victories in numerous matches and are a regular presence in West Bengal's Super 8 teams.

Kolkata Kiddathon- Round Table India

We proudly supported Round Table India's Kolkata Kiddathon 2024, promoting children's health and community spirit. The event featured a children's marathon and activities, raising funds for development of schools in rural Bengal.

Midnight Cycling Race- Round Table India

We supported the Midnight Cycling Race organized by Round Table India, demonstrating our commitment to community development. The event successfully raised funds to support local government schools in West Bengal. This initiative aims to improve educational infrastructure and provide essential resources, fostering a better learning environment for students.

Community Development -

To foster sustainable progress, our community development initiatives are rooted in the strong belief in empowering and uplifting communities. We are committed to providing equal opportunities and facilitating access to fundamental amenities, thereby enabling individuals to achieve self-sufficiency and build thriving futures. This commitment extends to addressing systemic inequalities that hinder progress and creating environments where all members of society can flourish.

AD MY BIN

We support Ad My Bin, an initiative that addresses critical waste disposal issues in India through installing disposal bins. As of now, we have installed over 100 bins in cities like Bhubaneshwar, Cuttack, Durgapur, Kolkata and Siliguri. The initiative also focuses on waste recycling and providing stable employment for waste collectors, offering 1 permanent job per 40 bins. We believe this model demonstrates how social responsibility and sustainable business can create a more environmentally conscious and equitable future.

SOS VILLAGE

To support vulnerable children at SOS Children's Village, we installed a reliable water tank, ensuring consistent clean water access. This simple act promises health and stability, fostering a nurturing environment. We hope this contributes to their brighter, more secure future, allowing them to thrive.
