Niznal® Trellising Wire

NIZNAL® Trellising Wire is a durable, long-lasting steel wire coated with a zinc-aluminium alloy for superior corrosion resistance. This advanced coating extends the wire's life up to 6 times compared to traditional galvanized wire. The wire's high tensile strength supports heavy crops, while its flexibility ensures easy installation. Available in various gauges and lengths, NIZNAL® Trellising Wire is ideal for a host of agricultural applications.

FEATURES

Composition

NIZNAL® Trellising Wire are made from premium steel wires coated with a 90% zinc and 10% aluminium alloy.

Diameter Range and Coating

NIZNAL® Trellising Wire are available in wires ranging from 1.4-7mm with coatings ranging from 70-650 GSM

Tensile Strength

NIZNAL® Trellising Wire display exceptional tensile strength ranging from 300-1900 MPa

Surface Finish

NIZNAL® Trellising Wire have a smooth and uniform surface finish that is free of defects or imperfections.

Standards

NIZNAL® Trellising Wire are made according to a range of international standards which include ASTM, BS and DIN.

Packaging

NIZNAL® Trellising Wire are packaged in 50 kg coils.

APPLICATIONS

Orchard Trellising

In orchards, NIZNAL® Trellising Wire offers reliable support for fruit-bearing branches of trees like apples, pears, and peaches. This prevents branch breakage under the weight of fruit, ensuring a bountiful harvest. By elevating the fruit, the wire reduces ground contact, minimising the risk of disease and pest infestation. This leads to higher-quality fruit and increased yield.

Vegetable Garden Trellising

NIZNAL® Trellising Wire is a versatile solution for supporting climbing vegetables like peas, beans, cucumbers, and tomatoes. By providing a vertical growing surface, it maximises space utilisation in the garden. The wire also keeps the fruits off the ground, preventing rot and promoting better air circulation and sunlight exposure. This results in healthier plants and a more abundant harvest.

Hop Trellising

In hop cultivation, NIZNAL® Trellising Wire offers a robust framework for hop vines to climb vertically. This maximises hop cone production and improves their quality. The wire's durability and flexibility facilitate efficient harvesting and drying processes, ensuring optimal yield and quality.

Flower Trellising

NIZNAL® Trellising Wire can be used to support climbing flowers like clematis, morning glories, and sweet peas. By providing a vertical structure, it creates visually appealing vertical gardens and enhances the overall landscape aesthetics. The wire also protects delicate flowers from damage and promotes better air circulation, leading to healthier and more vibrant blooms.

Berry Trellising

NIZNAL® Trellising Wire is an excellent choice for supporting berry bushes like raspberries, blackberries, and blueberries. It provides a sturdy framework, and helps keep the canes upright and prevent them from sprawling on the ground. This reduces the risk of fruit rot, pest infestation, and soil-borne diseases. Additionally, it improves air circulation and sunlight penetration within the berry patch, leading to healthier plants and higher-quality fruit.
