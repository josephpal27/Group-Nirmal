CSR

At Group Nirmal, we believe in building more than just products; we believe in building stronger communities. It's a conviction that guides us, especially in the areas surrounding our production units in Deulti, Khurda, and Bangalore. For us, our CSR initiatives are a personal commitment, a way to give back to the places we call home and contribute to the nation's progress. We believe in creating meaningful change through initiatives that touch lives directly and address unique community needs to create a lasting impact. We invite you to explore our initiatives below and discover the ways we are making a difference, and to join us in our journey of building a stronger, more sustainable tomorrow for India.

Our Initiatives

We strive continuously to support community well-being across a range of areas, with the hope of contributing to inclusive progress and resolving various issues. Through our efforts, we seek to address various challenges and promote equitable growth and upliftment, with the intent of building a foundation where more individuals can prosper and contribute to a resilient future.
