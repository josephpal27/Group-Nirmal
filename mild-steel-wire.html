MILD STEEL WIRE

Mild steel wires, made using low carbon steel, are renowned for their excellent ductility, tensile strength, and formability. They are available in various diameters and lengths and exhibit superior weldability and a smooth surface finish. Their consistent quality and cost-effectiveness make them a preferred choice for numerous industrial and construction applications.

FEATURES

Material

Mild steel wires are made from low carbon steel wire rods.

Standards

Mild steel wires conform to IS 280, ASTM A 510, JIS G-3505.

Coating

Mild steel wires are coated as drawn, phosphated, hot- dip galvanised, zinc Aluminum Coated. Coating thickness ranges between 40-600 GSM.

Wire diameter

Mild steel wires are available in various diameters ranging between 1.60-7.00 mm.

Tensile Strength

The tensile strength of mild steel wires ranges between 1130-3000 MPa.

APPLICATIONS

Sparkler Wire

Mild steel wire cores are used to manufacture sparklers. The wire is coated with a powdered mixture of various fuels that when lit, produce flames and sparks of various colours.

Cloth Hangers

Mild steel wire is bent and shaped to form sturdy and durable hangers for clothes. Its strength and malleability make it ideal for this application.

Chain Link Fence

Thick, galvanised mild steel wires are interlocked to create strong and secure chain-link fences. The wire's tensile strength and corrosion resistance make it suitable for outdoor use.

Cycle Spoke Wire

High-tensile strength mild steel wire is used to create the spokes of bicycle wheels. These spokes provide structural support and enable the wheel to rotate smoothly.

Weld Mesh Wire

Welded mild steel wire mesh is used in construction and agriculture for reinforcement, fencing, and screening. Its versatility and durability make it a popular choice for various applications.

Wire Mesh for Animal Cages

Mild steel wire mesh is commonly used to construct cages for animals like rabbits, guinea pigs, and birds. The mesh provides a secure enclosure while allowing for ventilation and visibility. It's durable, easy to clean, and can be customized to fit various cage sizes and shapes.
